About me

Hi, I’m Carlito. I’m a self-taught chef and designer from Madrid. I currently work in Switzerland as a self employet caterar and chef. I also write for several magazines and publications.

I’ve written three cookbooks and are now on the quest to create an extensive online database of recipes. I believe in using verified methods and reinventing when needed. A nice mix of old and new.

So I provide my website to you completely free, because I want to leave a positive impact, and I believe I can inspire others to create beautiful dishes and creations as well. If this resonates with you, please consider linking to this blog.
